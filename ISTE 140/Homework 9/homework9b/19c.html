The differences

There are differnt compression methods for images. Three examples are PNG, GIF, and JPEG. PNG and GIF display the image as it is, even if it doesn't have a background. JPEG always gives the image a background. JPEG is also the best compression method becasue it displays the most high quality image. The second most high qualtiy compression method is PNG, and GIF produces the least high quality image.
